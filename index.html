I am interested in graphical development, specifically shaders. I enjoy taking screenshots of video games and applying filters over them to make them more unique.

the first screenshot is one taken from the 2024 video game Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine 2 edited with my favorite filter, the Anisotropic Kuwahara filter, which is then dithered to create a more pixelated result.

Introduced in 1976 by Michihiko Kuwahara and his colleagues as part of a study on digital image enhancement at the Hitachi Central Research Laboratory in Japan.
